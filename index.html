Viewpoint Scoring
Per-domain scores are weighted averages of outlet bias ratings (AllSides, Ad Fontes Media), adjusted for reading recency and depth. Confidence bands use Wilson score interval; domains with <15 articles show "data too thin."
Bakshy et al. (2015); Guess et al. (2023)
Echo Chamber Metric
Echo score = 1 − normalized Shannon entropy of viewpoint distribution per domain. Range 0–100%: 0% = perfectly balanced; 100% = single-viewpoint diet. Domains weighted by article count in composite.
Cinelli et al. (2021, PNAS); Bail et al. (2018)
Coverage Gap Detection
Gaps identified by comparing your source cluster against a reference corpus by outlet category: wire, local, labor, expert, foreign-language, advocacy. Missing categories surface as personal gaps — not generic recommendations.
Robertson et al. (2023, UW); Altay et al. (2022)
